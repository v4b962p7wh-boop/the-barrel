Features

Built for people who take bourbon seriously.

Every feature in The Barrel is designed around how collectors actually think about their bottles.

📷
AI label scanning

Point your camera at any bottle. The Barrel identifies the distillery, age statement, proof, and mashbill in seconds — no typing required.

🏛️
Your vault

Track every bottle you own: purchase price, fill level, status, and personal rating. See your collection's total value at a glance.

📓
Dram log

Capture nose, palate, and finish with guided tasting notes. Tag flavors and build a personal tasting history across your entire collection.

🧬
Flavor DNA

Your tasting history becomes a palate profile. See which flavor categories you gravitate toward and which notes appear in your favorites.

👥
Community

Share tasting notes, post great finds, and follow collectors whose palates you trust. Like, comment, and build your circle.

🔄
Trade board

List bottles you're hunting or offering. Connect directly with other collectors — no middleman, no fees.

🥂
Tasting circles

Create invite-only groups for your tasting crew. Share notes just with the people you trade and drink with.

📅
Bourbon releases

Stay on top of upcoming releases from the distilleries you follow most.
